ABOUT US

Umax constructions was established in the year 2010. Since then, the company has been the instigating force behind building dream spaces for individuals and institutions. The company has evolved from strength to strength, and successfully completed numerous projects in residential and commercial sectors. Umax constructions has requisite expertise and wide-ranging experience to undertake construction projects with single source responsibility. Contracts are executed using state of the art design tools and project management techniques from concept to commissioning. We envisage a future wherein Umax constructions symbolises superior community living of the highest standards of quality and customer delight.
